Declaration

Naming the ideology. Defining the threat.

Antizionism is presented here as the modern ideological form of Jew-hatred: not ordinary policy criticism, but a worldview that denies Jewish peoplehood, recasts Jewish self-determination as uniquely illegitimate, and turns Israel into the symbolic villain of the present moral order.

The declaration function is practical. It gives schools, institutions, civic leaders, faith communities, and public organizations a clear standard for identifying when language has crossed from criticism into collective demonization.
